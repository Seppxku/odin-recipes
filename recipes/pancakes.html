Pancakes Recipe

Home

Description

Fluffy pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite, perfect for a cozy morning.

This easy recipe uses basic ingredients to create soft, golden pancakes that are light and airy. Serve them warm with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or your favorite toppings for a delicious start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (plus extra for cooking)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet ingredients
  3. Make the batter
  4. Heat the pan
  5. Cook the pancakes
  6. Flip and finish
  7. Serve warm
