Guacamole Recipe

Home

Description

This classic guacamole is a fresh, creamy, and zesty avocado dip that's perfect as a snack or appetizer.

Made with ripe avocados mashed with lime juice, garlic, and fresh herbs, it’s quick to prepare and packed with flavor. Whether you’re serving it with tortilla chips, tacos, or as a spread on sandwiches, this guacamole brings a bright and refreshing taste to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small lime (juiced)
  • 1 small garlic clove (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
  • 1 small tomato (deseeded and diced)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: a pinch of chili flakes or diced jalapeño for some heat

Steps

  1. Prepare the avocados
  2. Mash the avocado
  3. Add lime juice and garlic
  4. Mix in the vegetables and herbs
  5. Season to taste
  6. Serve immediately
