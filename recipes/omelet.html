Omelet Recipe

Home

Description

This classic omelette is quick, simple, and packed with protein — perfect for breakfast or a light meal. Made with just a few ingredients, it starts with fresh eggs whisked until fluffy, then poured into a hot, buttered pan.

As the eggs cook, they form a soft, golden base ready to be filled with your favorite ingredients like cheese, ham, vegetables, or herbs. Once set, the omelette is gently folded and served warm. Fluffy on the inside and slightly crisp on the edges, it’s a delicious and satisfying dish that you can customize to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil
  • Optionnal fillings :
    • 30g (1 oz) grated cheese (e.g., cheddar, Swiss)
    • 2–3 slices of ham or cooked bacon, chopped
    • ½ small onion, finely chopped
    • ½ bell pepper, diced
    • Fresh herbs (parsley, chives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Crack and beat the eggs
  2. Prepare the fillings (optional)
  3. Heat the pan
  4. Pour in the eggs
  5. Cook gently
  6. Add the fillings
  7. Fold and serve
